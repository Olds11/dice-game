How to Play

Game Rules:

  • 1. The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
  • 2. In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND ("current") score.
  • 3. BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • 4. The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his TOTAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • 5. The first player to reach 100 points on the TOTAL score wins the game.
